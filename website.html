About the Artist

The Story Behind the Brush

Hi, I'm Divya — a passionate bridal makeup artist based in Hyderabad, dedicated to making every woman feel like the most beautiful version of herself on her most special days.

With over 8 years of expertise and 500+ happy brides, I blend traditional South Indian bridal aesthetics with contemporary global trends to craft looks that are timeless, camera-ready, and uniquely yours.

I use only premium, skin-safe products and tailor every look to your skin tone, outfit, occasion and personality — because no two women are the same.
